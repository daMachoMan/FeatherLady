Why choose us?

Trained and Skilled Staff

We have a team of highly trained and experienced staff. Our staff is professional and go through our in house training. Our cleaning staffs have unique training to clean house environments and will be able to deliver cleaning results efficiently. All of our staff is reference checked and background checked so that you are confident in only hiring the best staff.

Tailored cleaning packages

We are eager to please our clients and we will listen to your cleaning requirements closely. We will work alongside you ensuring that you device a cleaning schedule that suits your business. We are flexible and ready to work inside and outside our business hours and we are able to mobilise our teams if extra work is required. We will also remain adaptable and will try changing our schedule to suit your needs.

Cleaning products & Equipment

While some cleaning companies travel with equipment from one job to another, we advise against it. This way there is no transfer of dirt, germs, allergens, or debris from one house to the next. In order for us to maintain our high cleaning standards, we require that all our clients have their own equipment. We provide non- toxic cleaning products at an extra cost, and provide our clients with an easy obtainable list of equipment. Equipment list can be discussed with one of our client care specialists based on your home, and we will only recommend the best and most important cleaning equipment for you. No time to shop for equipment? No problem! One of our client care specialist can assist you
