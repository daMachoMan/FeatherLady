About

We are Featherlady Cleaning Company. We specialise in cleaning houses. We have over 6 years of experience providing high quality cleaning services.

We were inspired to start a cleaning company after years of working within the care sector and realising that some of the elderly clients we worked with needed help with cleaning and maintaining their properties. We joined age concern services listing and through providing our trustworthy and reliable service we received a lot of referrals and a good reputation. Our services became popular and were being used by families, landlords and other business.

We are committed to the satisfaction of our customers and with our passion of cleaning we believe it is our duty to make sure all our customers are delivered with good quality cleaning.

Each and every one of our clients is very important to us therefor in a professional manner we always make sure we communicate and create a good working relationship with our clients. We always aim to meet every individuals cleaning requirements of all our customers that are why we carry out an initial free visit for cleaning so you can tell us what you would like us to do and we try our best to achieve it

Our Vision

Making a noticeable difference to the business we serve, the communities in which we operate, and the staff which we employ.

Our Mission

We thrive to provide a good quality cleaning service of high standard making sure all our customers are satisfied. We aim to keep encouraging our customers to live in a clean environment for their physical and mental well-being.

Our Values

Respect: Behaving positively and professionally.

Loyalty: A duty of care for our staff, customers and the environment.

Integrity: Providing a trustworthy and honesty service and do what we say will do.

Excellence: To always look to provide the best quality experience with regards to our cleaning and our customer service.
