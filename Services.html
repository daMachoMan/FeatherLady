House Cleaning

Your home is a reflection of who you are as a person. An organized home with a superior clean from Feather Lady will increase your sense of wellbeing and impress those visiting your home.

Supported Living Cleaning

What if you had a cleaning service that cared about cleaning as much as did about client care? We understand that cleaning in the healthcare sector in critical with CQC – that it can’t just look clean, it has to be clean?

Feather Lady provides a good quality service in maintaining supported living accommodations so that the residents really feel like they are in a home.

Our cleaning teams for supported living accommodation are carefully selected checked and trained to understand the people who will be living in the building they clean. We maintain the same team of cleaners in the building so that the residents get to know their cleaner and the cleaner gets to know the people who live in the building they are providing a cleaning service for. DBS checked cleaners are available where this is appropriate.
